description: Drives Stitch shot animation (image-to-video). Edit to change how every still becomes motion.

Animate the still with subtle, believable motion only: a slow push or drift, faint parallax, and ambient life — breath, a flicker of light, drifting hair or smoke. Hold the composition, identity, wardrobe, and palette of the reference frame exactly. No new characters, no scene changes, no morphing or warping. Cinematic, steady, and restrained, about five seconds.

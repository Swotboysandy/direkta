# MAURYA ORIGINS — 2-Min Trailer (Screenplay Test)

**Focus:** Chandragupta + Chanakya
**Trailer spine:** Tyranny → Discovery → Training → Rebellion → Empire

> Background context: the wider world (Alexander, Porus, the fear of Dhana Nanda) is set up
> elsewhere; this trailer uses that only as background and pivots strongly toward Chanakya and
> Chandragupta. Dhana Nanda is established as the greater eastern threat.

---

## 0:00-0:10

### BLACK SCREEN

Low, distant sound of marching feet.

A faint chant rises.

**VOICEOVER - OLD MAN**
In Bharat, kingdoms rise by sword…

Cut to flashes:

A burning village.
Tax collectors dragging sacks of grain.
A crying mother clutching her child.
Soldiers bearing the seal of Magadha.

**VOICEOVER - OLD MAN**
…but empires are born from suffering.

Hard cut to:

---

## 0:10-0:25

### EXT. MAGADHA MARKET - DAY

The streets are crowded, tense, hungry.

Nanda soldiers beat a farmer to the ground. The crowd watches in fear.

A young boy, **CHANDRAGUPTA**, watches from the shadows. His fists tighten.

A soldier kicks over a basket of food.

**NANDA SOLDIER**
The king owns your land. Your grain. Your breath.

Chandragupta steps forward.

An older woman grabs his arm.

**OLDER WOMAN**
Not now.

Chandragupta pulls away.

**CHANDRAGUPTA**
Then when?

The soldier turns.

A sharp slap sends Chandragupta to the ground.

The crowd lowers its eyes.

But Chandragupta does not.

---

## 0:25-0:40

### INT. ROYAL COURT OF DHANA NANDA - DAY

Gold everywhere. Excess. Silence.

**DHANA NANDA** sits on the throne, amused as ministers bow.

A scholar enters. Thin. Sharp-eyed. Unbending.

This is **CHANAKYA**.

The court laughs at his appearance.

Dhana Nanda leans forward.

**DHANA NANDA**
A Brahmin comes to teach a king?

Chanakya does not bow.

**CHANAKYA**
No. I came to warn one.

The court falls silent.

**CHANAKYA**
A throne built on fear does not stand forever.

Dhana Nanda's smile vanishes.

---

## 0:40-0:52

### EXT. PALACE STEPS - DAY

Chanakya is thrown out of the palace.

His scrolls scatter across the stone.

The court laughs behind him.

Chanakya slowly rises.

Blood at his lip.

He ties his loosened hair into a knot.

**CHANAKYA**
I will not untie this…

Close on his eyes.

**CHANAKYA**
…until the Nandas are uprooted.

---

## 0:52-1:05

### EXT. FOREST OUTSKIRTS - EVENING

Chandragupta runs through the woods, chased by soldiers.

He moves fast, instinctive, fearless.

He ducks under a spear, grabs a branch, trips one soldier, and vanishes behind trees.

Chanakya watches from a distance.

Not impressed by strength.

Impressed by instinct.

A soldier corners Chandragupta.

Before the blade falls, Chanakya's staff strikes the soldier's wrist.

The sword drops.

Chandragupta looks at Chanakya.

**CHANDRAGUPTA**
Who are you?

**CHANAKYA**
The man who saw what you are.

---

## 1:05-1:20

### EXT. RIVERBANK - NIGHT

A fire burns between them.

Chandragupta eats like someone who has known hunger.

Chanakya studies him.

**CHANAKYA**
You have anger.

**CHANDRAGUPTA**
I have reason.

**CHANAKYA**
Anger burns a man. Reason builds a kingdom.

Chandragupta looks up.

**CHANDRAGUPTA**
I don't want a kingdom.

Chanakya leans closer.

**CHANAKYA**
That is why you may deserve one.

---

## 1:20-1:35

### TRAINING MONTAGE

Fast cuts:

Chandragupta learning swordplay.

Chanakya drawing maps in the dirt.

Chandragupta falling, bleeding, rising again.

Chanakya placing stones on a map.

Villagers whispering.

Hidden rebels gathering.

A blacksmith forging blades.

Nanda spies watching from rooftops.

**CHANAKYA - VOICEOVER**
A king is not made by bloodline.

Chandragupta disarms a trained warrior.

**CHANAKYA - VOICEOVER**
He is made by discipline.

Chandragupta studies battle formations.

**CHANAKYA - VOICEOVER**
By sacrifice.

A village secretly pledges loyalty.

**CHANAKYA - VOICEOVER**
By the people who choose to follow him.

---

## 1:35-1:47

### INT. NANDA COURT - NIGHT

A spy kneels before Dhana Nanda.

**SPY**
There are whispers, Maharaj.

Dhana Nanda drinks from a golden cup.

**DHANA NANDA**
Whispers die.

The spy hesitates.

**SPY**
This one has a name.

Dhana Nanda looks up.

**SPY**
Chandragupta.

The cup stops halfway to Dhana Nanda's mouth.

---

## 1:47-1:56

### EXT. HILLTOP OVERLOOKING MAGADHA - DAWN

Chandragupta and Chanakya stand together.

Below them: the vast capital of Magadha.

Chandragupta is no longer just a boy. He carries himself like a leader.

**CHANDRAGUPTA**
How do two men fight an empire?

Chanakya looks at the rising sun.

**CHANAKYA**
We don't fight it.

Beat.

**CHANAKYA**
We make it collapse from within.

---

## 1:56-2:00

### FINAL MONTAGE

A rebel army raises hidden banners.

Nanda soldiers charge.

Chanakya's eyes burn with purpose.

Chandragupta rides forward on horseback.

A sword is lifted against the sunrise.

Cut to black.

### TITLE CARD:

**MAURYA ORIGINS**

### FINAL LINE OVER BLACK:

**CHANAKYA - VOICEOVER**
Before a Maurya sits on the throne…
a Nanda must fall.

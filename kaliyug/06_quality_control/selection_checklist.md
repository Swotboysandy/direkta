# Image Selection and Rejection Checklist

## Must Pass
- 16:9 landscape aspect ratio
- sharp, clean, high-detail image
- cinematic realistic fantasy style
- no pixelation or muddy compression
- strong lighting and readable composition
- usable as a concept-trailer keyframe
- world-first composition unless the plate explicitly calls for a character or title card
- no generated text overlays except the final KALIYUG title card

## Real-World Scene Rules
Real-world scenes must include post-apocalyptic destruction, dark fantasy atmosphere, red demonic corruption where appropriate, vein-like organic growths merging with structures in corrupted areas, and physical grime such as ash, smoke, rust, mud, broken stone, bone, and torn cloth.

Real-world scenes must avoid sacred geometry, floating symbols, holograms, cosmic mandalas, and neon sci-fi UI.

Characters in real-world plates should usually be small scale markers, omens, or environmental punctuation. Reject if a world-first plate becomes a clean character poster.

## Dream-Realm Scene Rules
Dream scenes may include sacred geometry, cosmic mandalas, floating symbols, impossible architecture, and blue-violet-magenta divine light.

Dream scenes should still feel realistic fantasy, not generic sci-fi wallpaper.

## Kali Rules
Reject immediately if the image depicts the villain as Goddess Kali, divine feminine villain, tongue-out goddess, multi-armed goddess, or Maa Kali iconography.

Accept if Kali influence appears as asuric shadow, demonic rot, vice/gambling/dice symbolism, diseased gold, red-black veins, corrupted thrones, broken dharma, dark propaganda.

## Kalki Rules
First-half Kalki: young seeker, rugged, uncertain, observant, not armored, not superheroic.

Second-half Kalki: blue-white divine presence, burdened expression, Brahma Kavach aura, Pinaka weapon, Sharanga chakra-shield, grounded mythic feel.

## Kaka Rules
Kaka must be a realistic crow: ancient, intelligent, serious, never cute or comedic.

## Raavan Rules
Raavan should be colossal, terrifying, demon emperor, soot-black/diseased-gold armor, ember red eyes, with many-headed nature suggested using shadow/smoke if needed.

Reject if Raavan looks cartoonish, theatrical/campy, plastic CGI, or too human-sized for climax shots.

## High-Priority Visual Target
The selected image set should move like a mythic world tour: ruined Bharat, poisoned sacred geography, red demonic corruption, pure Kailash sanctuary, human remnants, Kali's systems of vice and power, vision and dream revelation, blue-white awakening as environmental force, colossal red-black antagonist war, and finally blue-white Kalki vs red-black Raavan confrontation.

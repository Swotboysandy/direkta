# Kaliyug Trailer Visual Bible — Locked Version

> **SUPERSEDED (project-wide) by `kaliyug_game_art_direction.md`** — the Soulsborne / Victorian-Gothic +
> Lovecraftian, Indo-Gothic, reimagined-deity art direction is now the **project-wide** look. This file
> is kept for **reference** as the earlier grounded, PS5-realistic **trailer** look; the trailer is to
> be **re-cut** to the game art direction. Where they differ, the game art direction governs.

## 1. Master Style

**Style:** realistic fantasy, cinematic, dark, mythic, post-apocalyptic.

The trailer should look like a grounded dark-fantasy epic after World War 3, where Indian sacred architecture, ruined modern civilization, demonic corruption, and spiritual destiny collide.

Do not make it look like clean sci-fi, cyberpunk neon city, superhero fantasy, anime, cartoon mythology, glossy fantasy kingdom, or colorful divine spectacle.

## 2. Real World Rule

The real world must be desolate, frightening, post-apocalyptic, gory in atmosphere, dark-magic infected, red themed when corrupted, and filled with vein-like demonic growths branching through structures.

Use physical textures: ash, cracked stone, rust, mud, blood-stained cloth, bone, soot, burnt wood, dead roots, black ichor, and glowing red cracks.

### Real World Must Avoid
- sacred geometry in the sky
- floating mandalas
- cosmic diagrams
- holograms
- luminous abstract symbols
- clean metaphysical portals

## 3. Dream Realm Rule

Sacred geometry, cosmic mandalas, floating symbols, impossible architecture, and metaphysical diagrams belong only in dreams, visions, and spiritual-revelation sequences.

Dream realm should feel like the hidden order behind reality: cosmic, beautiful, overwhelming, and dangerous.

## 4. Kali Correction

Kali is **not Goddess Kali**.

Kali means the demon/personification of Kali Yuga: decay, conflict, corruption, vice, greed, falsehood, manipulation, gambling, violence, and systemic rot.

### Never Depict Kali As
- Goddess Kali
- divine feminine villain
- multi-armed goddess
- tongue-out goddess iconography
- Maa Kali silhouette
- Shakti wrath imagery

### Correct Kali Visual Motifs
- asuric shadow intelligence
- black-red demonic rot
- branching veins merging with architecture
- diseased gold
- dice / gambling symbolism
- broken dharma wheels
- corrupt kings and tyrants
- propaganda banners
- blood-red storms
- dark thrones
- infected temples
- ritual smoke
- demonic male or abstract monstrous forms

## 5. Red Demonic Corruption Direction

Use crimson sky glow, black-red organic veins, vein roots crawling up towers, wet black ichor, glowing red cracks, flesh-like demonic growths fused into stone and metal, corrupted temple spires wrapped in red-black tendrils, blood-red fog, burning ritual pits, bone fragments, and skulls as environmental details.

The corruption should look like civilization itself has become infected tissue.

## 6. Protagonist / Kalki Direction

### First Half
Kalki is a seeker, not yet fully aware of his identity.

Look: young Indian male, rough survival clothing, ash-stained skin, torn shawl or cloak, practical cloth wraps, lean, alert, emotionally burdened, not royal, not superheroic, not fully armored.

### Second Half
After realization, Kalki manifests divine force.

Look: blue-white divine aura, serious burdened expression, Brahma Kavach as luminous protective armor aura, Pinaka as transforming divine bow/spear/blade, Sharanga as chakra-shield, powerful but grounded realistic fantasy.

## 7. Kaka Direction

Kaka is a serious guardian crow: realistic black crow, intelligent eyes, blue-black feather sheen, ancient, still, watchful, never cute, never comedic.

## 8. Raavan Direction

Raavan appears in the second half as a massive antagonist and final confrontation threat.

Look: colossal demon emperor, giant battlefield scale, soot-black and diseased-gold armor, ember red eyes, ancient crown, terrifying warlord presence. His many-headed nature may be suggested by ghostly faces in smoke/shadow rather than cluttered cartoon literalism.

## 9. Palette

### Real World
Crimson red, black, soot gray, ash gray, rust, dark maroon, dried blood, diseased gold, dull bone white.

### Sanctuary
Cold blue, slate gray, snow white, pine green, clear river cyan, natural sunlight/moonlight.

### Dream Realm
Deep indigo, cosmic black, blue-violet, magenta, white-gold sacred light, cyan geometry.

## 10. Cinematography

Use wide establishing shots, slow push-ins, low-angle mythic compositions, over-the-shoulder seeker framing, heavy atmospheric lighting, volumetric smoke, detailed realistic textures, and 16:9 cinematic frame.

Avoid shaky cam overuse, clean poster symmetry in every shot, over-bright fantasy, and superhero poses until the late climax.

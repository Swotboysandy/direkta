# 03 · Indo-Gothic / Indo-Saracenic Colonial

The middle stratum — the **dead modern state**, now a warlord court. Real historical fusion of European Gothic + Indian forms.

## Pulls
- **Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj Terminus / Victoria Terminus, Mumbai** — the definitive Victorian-Gothic + Indian fusion: pointed arches, ribbed vaults, gargoyles **+** Indian domes, chhatris, carved stone. https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Chhatrapati_Shivaji_Maharaj_Terminus
- **Bombay High Court · University of Mumbai (Rajabai Clock Tower)** — colonial Gothic civic monuments.
- **Indo-Saracenic** (Madras/Chennai, Mysore Palace, Muir College) — domes, cusped arches, minarets fused with Gothic. https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Indo-Saracenic_architecture

## What to pull for KALIYUG
- **Pointed arch + Indian dome / chhatri / carving + gargoyles** — the specific fusion (NOT a European castle, NOT "India cosplaying London").
- Recontextualize it: the colonial Indo-Gothic layer is **a wound on Bharat** — a fallen order over older sacred stone — not decoration.
- **Kali's court** = a CSMT-style civic hall corrupted: black stone + diseased gold, defaced carvings, the **empty throne directly under the dome** (the upward sacred axis hollowed into tyranny).
- Gargoyles → corrupted guardian **yaksha / gana**; flying buttresses → temple-spire ribs; wrought iron → rusted **trishul-fencing** with bells.

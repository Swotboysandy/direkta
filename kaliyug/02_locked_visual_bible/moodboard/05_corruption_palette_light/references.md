# 05 · Corruption, Palette & Light

Grotesque-yet-elegant corruption, the disciplined palette, and the light grammar.

## The locked palette (one accent per real-world frame)
- **Gothic ground:** Soot/ash black `#171311` · ash grey `#6E6A66` · rust/dried-blood maroon `#5C2A1E` · sodium-lamp amber · oxidized-bronze green.
- **Transition (sickening):** **diseased gold `#9C7B2E`** (sickly, never rich) · bile-yellow · verdigris · dried blood.
- **Cosmic / Blood-Moon:** crimson `#8B1A1A` · bruise-violet & abyssal indigo (`#5B3A8C`) · an oily iridescent **"colour out of space"** (petrol/peacock) that reads *wrong*.
- **Sacred counter-accent:** divine blue-white `#BFE3F2` (relic-pulse, awakening aura, the thread).
- **Sanctuary:** cold blue / river cyan `#3E7C8C` · snow-white · pine-green — beautiful, never cozy.

## Corruption & light pulls
- **Corruption as malignant ornament** — sinuous black-red veins echoing **sacred carving gone wrong**, never random gore; flesh fused into stone; **rotten-opulent diseased gold** (wealth as disease).
- **The Blood Moon (raktachandra)** hanging too large, too low.
- **Fog / volumetric ritual smoke** hiding the tops of monumental ruins; jali god-light shafts.
- Art references: **Bloodborne** palette discipline; **Tsutomu Nihei** (architecture-as-horror, scale); **Zdzisław Beksiński** (sublime decay); **Wayne Barlowe** (alien-sublime).

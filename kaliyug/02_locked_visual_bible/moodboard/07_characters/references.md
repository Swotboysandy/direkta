# 07 · Characters

Grounded real-world figures (Kalki, Kaka, Vasuki, Raavan) + abstract Kali. **No calendar-art; Souls-grade grounded dark design.**

## Pulls
- **KALKI** — *first half:* a burdened young Indian **seeker** — lean, alert, ash-grimed survival cloth + torn shawl, no armour, no glow (a Souls protagonist, not a superhero). *Second half:* blue-white **divine aura**, **Brahma Kavach** as a luminous armour-*aura* (not metal), **Pinaka** (transforming bow/spear/blade of light and ash), **Sharanga** chakra-shield. Hope is **costly**, his face grief not glory.
- **KALI** — **abstract from the start** (per founder): bodiless, systemic — an **empty diseased-gold throne**, dice on a war-map, **red-black smoke that moves like thought**, a layered whisper. Escalates to a **Vishvarupa-scale cosmic vastness** (the age itself is the Old One). NEVER Goddess Kali, never a horned warlord figure.
- **KAKA** — a serious, ancient, watchful **black crow** (blue-black sheen, intelligent eyes); cosmic stillness, never comedic. *(His true nature — Kakabhushundi — stays sealed.)*
- **VASUKI** — a vast, ancient cosmic **serpent**, eyes like cold stars, coiled around **Pinaka** in the drained baoli/cavern; guardian who tests by stillness.
- **RAAVAN** — a colossal demon-emperor **2-second tease**: soot-black + diseased-gold armour, ember eyes, ancient crown, many faces only *suggested* in smoke. A sting, not a reveal.

## What to pull
- Souls/Bloodborne **boss-grade awe-terror** for Vasuki & Raavan; grounded survival-realism for Kalki; abstract/atmospheric staging for Kali (he is never a body).

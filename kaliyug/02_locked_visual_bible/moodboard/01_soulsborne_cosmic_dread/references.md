# 01 · Soulsborne & Cosmic Dread

The **mood, scale, light, and palette discipline.** Drop reference frames here.

## Pulls
- **Bloodborne** — Yharnam's gothic decay; the **Blood Moon**; the gothic→cosmic arc; Great Ones as awe-and-terror (the *template* for "the age is the Old One"). https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Bloodborne
- **Elden Ring** — the Erdtree's monumental sacred scale; the **never-seen Outer Gods** (awe without a hero-shot); desaturation so one sacred colour reads. https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Elden_Ring
- **Dark Souls** — the **interconnected vertical world** that loops back (not corridors); the **bonfire** as the single warm anchor of light. https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Dark_Souls
- **Shadow of the Colossus** — divinity = scale so vast it "cannot have been made by humans"; the tiny rider beneath the colossus.

## What to pull for KALIYUG
- **Dread as default; beauty as grief** — never cozy. Rare beauty mourns what's lost.
- **Monumental scale that dwarfs the player** — architecture leaning over a tiny Kalki; tops lost in red fog.
- **Single warm/sacred light source per frame** (the "bonfire" → the cracked blue-white relic, a self-ringing bell, a lone cavern shaft) and the **one hair-thin blue-white thread** through the dark.
- **Set-pieces as sacred dread** — stillness → held breath → reveal (Vasuki, the empty throne, Raavan's ember eyes).
- **Desaturated near-monochrome ground; one accent detonates** (red OR blue-white, not both).

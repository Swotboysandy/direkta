# 06 · Reimagined Deities (dream realm only)

**Cosmic beings, still shaped as the deity** — recognizable as Shiva, Vishnu, Lakshmi, etc., but their *aesthetic* completely reimagined into an original dark-cosmic sublime. **Reimagine how they're perceived; keep the identity. Negate calendar-art style, never the god.** The numinous counter-pole to Kali — clean, vast, no gore. *(This is the only category where sacred geometry / cosmic imagery is allowed.)*

## Treatment references (the *how*)
- **Elden Ring** — awe and scale (apply it to a *recognizable* figure, not a never-seen blob).
- **Shadow of the Colossus** — divinity through impossible scale and overwhelming light.
- **Hellblade: Senua's Sacrifice** — the numinous / the divine bleeding into perception.
- **The Vishvarupa** (Gita 11) — Bharat's own "Great One" template: the universal form, mouths and faces past count — recognizable as Krishna, rendered cosmic.

## Per-deity (form kept, aesthetic transfigured)
- **Lakshmi** (Grace) — golden caustics / liquid light / fertile bioluminescence; blooming lotus-mandala.
- **Ram** (Duty) — clean architectural dawn-light; a single unwavering luminous axis / bridge of light.
- **Saraswati & Brahma** (Knowledge) — sound made visible (cymatics); white starfield resolving into pattern; four-fold radial recursion.
- **Krishna** (Self-realisation) — iridescent oilslick / peacock-spectrum interference; a turning wheel of time. **Never** flute-and-cowherd calendar art.
- **Shiva & Parvati** (Perception) — ash-ember **event-horizon** fused with living stone; the Ardhanarishvara half-dark/half-molten; the **third eye** as an aperture of burning light that *sees*.
- **Vishnu** (Acceptance) — cold still **orbital blue**; the reclining cosmic Narayana barely resolving from the dark; the navel-lotus a slow toroidal bloom (the tenth petal = Kalki's face).

## Generation negatives (style, not identity)
`no poster/calendar god-art, no festival saffron-and-gold kitsch, no flat literal devotional illustration, no generic Hindu-deity stock art` — keep the god **recognizable**, force an original cosmic reimagining.

# 04 · Stepwells & Descent (the down-axis)

Verticality is theology — the spire rises to the divine; the **baoli descends** toward water / the underworld / serpent-scale things (Vasuki). KALIYUG's signature non-euclidean reveal.

## Pulls
- **Rani-ki-vav, Patan** — the masterwork inverted-temple stepwell; descending tiers of carved galleries. https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Rani_ki_vav
- **Chand Baori, Abhaneri** — vertiginous geometric lattice of descending stairs (the dread of depth). https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Chand_Baori
- **Adalaj Stepwell** — deep, pillared, light-shafted descent.

## What to pull for KALIYUG
- **Inverted-ziggurat descent** — stairs folding down into chthonic dark; dead silt at the bottom.
- **The signature reveal:** a baoli that descends **past where the water should be, into a star-field abyss** (the dream/cosmic bleed at the threshold of the real).
- **Vasuki's cavern-shrine** and the **claiming of Pinaka** stage here — serpent-scale set-piece in the drained dark, a lone cold shaft of light, Shiva-marks carved in living rock.
- Descent = deeper into corruption **and** truth (the Souls down-into-the-world structure).

# Kaliyug Standalone World-Flow Keyframe Prompts

All prompts below are standalone. They are designed for a 3-minute world-first concept trailer made from animated still frames. Generate 16:9 landscape images at the highest available quality, targeting clean 4K detail.

Characters are secondary unless a prompt explicitly says otherwise. The world, scale, atmosphere, and mythic geography are the focus. Leave clean negative space for captions where useful, but do not generate text overlays except for the final title card.

# Kaliyug Concept Trailer World Flow v2

## Runtime
Approx. 3 minutes.

## Format
This trailer is a world-first concept trailer built from animated still frames. Each frame should feel like a cinematic visual plate that can hold for 5-8 seconds with slow camera movement, smoke, fire, drifting ash, storm motion, water motion, or light movement.

The trailer should not behave like a plot-beat scene sequence. It should introduce the concept by showing the world, its ages, its corrupted systems, its sacred counterforces, and only then brief glimpses of characters.

Characters are secondary. The world is the main subject.

## Structural Flow

| Section | Time | Focus |
|---|---:|---|
| The Age Has Fallen | 0:00-0:35 | Post-war Bharat, poisoned civilization, ruined sacred geography. |
| The Last Pure Cradle | 0:35-0:55 | Kailash sanctuary and the preserved seed of renewal. |
| Humanity After Collapse | 0:55-1:20 | Survivors, pilgrimage roads, temple settlements, moral decay. |
| Kali's Age | 1:20-1:50 | Systemic corruption, vice, infected courts, red-black demonic spread. |
| The Vision Reveals the Pattern | 1:50-2:15 | Dream realm, sacred geometry, fragments of identity. |
| The Final Age Awakens | 2:15-2:50 | Blue-white divine force, armies, Raavan, world-scale confrontation. |
| Title | 2:50-3:00 | Kaliyug title card. |

## Visual Plate List

### Plate 01 - Ash Dawn Over Bharat
- **Time:** 0:00-0:07
- **Purpose:** Establish the fallen world before any character.
- **Visual:** Vast aerial of a ruined Indian city along a dead river, collapsed temples and modern infrastructure under ash-gold dawn.

### Plate 02 - The Poisoned Sacred River
- **Time:** 0:07-0:14
- **Purpose:** Show spiritual geography corrupted by the age.
- **Visual:** Polluted ghats, broken shrine steps, dead boats, black-red veins reaching into the riverbank.

### Plate 03 - Highways Become Ruins
- **Time:** 0:14-0:21
- **Purpose:** Merge modern collapse with mythic decay.
- **Visual:** Broken highways, rusted machines, temple spires, smoke columns, survivors as tiny scale markers.

### Plate 04 - Red Veins Through Civilization
- **Time:** 0:21-0:28
- **Purpose:** Define the demonic infection as environmental language.
- **Visual:** Red-black organic corruption branching through stone, asphalt, machines, and ruined sacred architecture.

### Plate 05 - Survivor Fires Under Broken Towers
- **Time:** 0:28-0:35
- **Purpose:** Show that humans remain, small and fragile.
- **Visual:** Tiny survivor camps glowing beneath immense ruined temple towers and overpasses.

### Plate 06 - The Last Pure Valley
- **Time:** 0:35-0:42
- **Purpose:** Contrast the fallen world with the preserved Kailash sanctuary.
- **Visual:** Hidden Himalayan valley, pure river, pine forest, snow peaks, clean cold light.

### Plate 07 - The Child and the Crow
- **Time:** 0:42-0:49
- **Purpose:** Introduce the mystery of Kalki without making him the trailer's center.
- **Visual:** A child or young boy small in frame near water, watched by a serious black crow in the sanctuary.

### Plate 08 - Boundary of Two Worlds
- **Time:** 0:49-0:56
- **Purpose:** Show the threshold between preservation and ruin.
- **Visual:** Mountain pass with pure sanctuary behind and dark corrupted valley ahead; crow leading forward.

### Plate 09 - Pilgrim Road of Ash
- **Time:** 0:56-1:03
- **Purpose:** Bring the wanderer into the wider ruined world.
- **Visual:** Tiny young wanderer and crow on an ash road through broken pilgrimage markers and distant infected temples.

### Plate 10 - Temple Settlement of the Remnant
- **Time:** 1:03-1:10
- **Purpose:** Establish human stakes through environment.
- **Visual:** Survivor settlement inside a ruined temple courtyard, fires, cloth shelters, wounded people, old stone.

### Plate 11 - Machine Shrine
- **Time:** 1:10-1:17
- **Purpose:** Show lost knowledge becoming myth.
- **Visual:** Survivors worship or scavenge around a dead war machine fused with a broken shrine.

### Plate 12 - The Relic Recoils
- **Time:** 1:17-1:24
- **Purpose:** Give the first divine clue as an environmental reaction.
- **Visual:** A cracked relic causes nearby red-black corruption to pull away; Kalki is present but not heroic.

### Plate 13 - Valley of Cult Fires
- **Time:** 1:24-1:31
- **Purpose:** Show Kali's influence through mass behavior.
- **Visual:** Ritual valley with cursed fires, broken dharma wheel, cult figures, black-red veins in structures.

### Plate 14 - Markets of Vice
- **Time:** 1:31-1:38
- **Purpose:** Show corruption as systems and incentives, not only monsters.
- **Visual:** Diseased-gold bazaar/court of gambling, debt, propaganda banners, armed tyrants, desperate crowds.

### Plate 15 - Kali's Throne Without a Body
- **Time:** 1:38-1:45
- **Purpose:** Reveal Kali as asuric intelligence and systemic rot, never Goddess Kali.
- **Visual:** Empty black stone and diseased-gold throne, dice on a blood-stained war map, red-black smoke like thought.

### Plate 16 - The Infected Court
- **Time:** 1:45-1:52
- **Purpose:** Introduce human agents of the age.
- **Visual:** Corrupt king, warlord, priest, masked assassin, monstrous general in a ruined citadel consumed by veins.

### Plate 17 - Forest of Black Roots
- **Time:** 1:52-1:59
- **Purpose:** Show corruption entering nature.
- **Visual:** Dead forest or jungle road, hanging roots, bone-white trunks, red-black growths, one small torch-lit path.

### Plate 18 - The Dream Gate Opens
- **Time:** 1:59-2:06
- **Purpose:** Pivot into the dream/vision realm where sacred geometry is allowed.
- **Visual:** Floating black stone platform in a star void, cosmic temple fragments, luminous sacred geometry gate.

### Plate 19 - Archive of Possible Selves
- **Time:** 2:06-2:13
- **Purpose:** Suggest Kalki's hidden identity through images, not exposition.
- **Visual:** Dream mirrors showing child, seeker, warrior, avatar fragments; crow shadow crossing cosmic floor.

### Plate 20 - The Hidden Order of Reality
- **Time:** 2:13-2:20
- **Purpose:** Reveal the hidden cosmic order behind the age.
- **Visual:** Vast cosmic chamber of mandalas and sacred geometry, star oceans, blue-violet-magenta light.

### Plate 21 - Blue-White Force in Ash
- **Time:** 2:20-2:27
- **Purpose:** Return to the real world with awakening energy.
- **Visual:** Ruined battlefield landscape where red veins recoil from a blue-white divine eruption around a kneeling figure.

### Plate 22 - Corruption Breaks Around the Sanctuary Road
- **Time:** 2:27-2:34
- **Purpose:** Show divine force as world effect.
- **Visual:** A road or river channel clearing through red-black infection, ash and smoke pulled back by blue-white light.

### Plate 23 - Raavan Behind the Burning Fortress
- **Time:** 2:34-2:41
- **Purpose:** Escalate to mythic antagonist scale.
- **Visual:** Colossal Raavan rising behind a fortress, smoke suggesting many faces, armies scattered below.

### Plate 24 - Army of the Final Age
- **Time:** 2:41-2:48
- **Purpose:** Show scale of the war, not individual combat.
- **Visual:** Kali-aligned army crossing a dead ash plain beneath red-black storm clouds and infected siege machines.

### Plate 25 - Small Kalki Before Giant Raavan
- **Time:** 2:48-2:55
- **Purpose:** Final world-scale confrontation image.
- **Visual:** Tiny awakened Kalki with blue-white aura facing colossal Raavan across a ruined battlefield.

### Plate 26 - Title Card
- **Time:** 2:55-3:00
- **Purpose:** End on the brand/title.
- **Visual:** KALIYUG carved in burnt stone and metal, ash drifting, red-black smoke, faint blue-white light.

## Caption Rule
The Varanasi reference uses chapter/location captions, but for generation the keyframes should usually avoid embedded text. Leave clean negative space where captions could be added later in editing. The only frame that should generate actual text is Plate 26, the title card.

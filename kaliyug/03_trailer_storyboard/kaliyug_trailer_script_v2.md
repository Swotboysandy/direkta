# KALIYUG — AAA Cinematic Trailer Script (v2, fresh cut)

> **Draft — 2026-06-24.** Fresh ~2-minute AAA game cinematic trailer (PS5 / Unreal ultra
> semi-realistic, grounded dark mythic). Hybrid **world → hero** structure, built from the cleaned
> Kaliyuga/Pathways canon. Features the **Pathways cosmic dream**, **Vasuki guarding Pinaka**,
> and the **Raavan tease**. Canon-locked: grounded real world; **Kali = demon of the age** (never
> Goddess Kali); sacred geometry only in dreams/visions; **Kaka** kept a plain, watchful crow (his
> true nature stays sealed). Pending founder review → next step: format into a parse-ready screenplay.

---

**LOGLINE:** An old sage's prophecy speaks itself into being — line by line it summons the fallen world, until the last verse demands that the age which crowned itself be unmade by the one child it forgot to poison.

**RUNTIME:** ~2:00 | 16:9 | PS5 / Unreal-engine ultra semi-realistic, grounded dark mythic.

---

### 0:00-0:07 — The Prophecy Begins (Black / Bell)
**Visual:** Pure black. A single temple bell sways into frame, barely lit through drifting ash. Red-black veins creep across its cracked bronze like blood finding its way into stone. A faint blue-white line of light flickers once in the dark — then is swallowed.
**Audio:** Sub-bass drone, almost felt not heard. One slow, cracked bell strike that decays too long. Distant thunder.
**VO / Old Sage (prophetic, weathered, low):** "It was written... that an age would forget it was ending."

### 0:07-0:15 — The Foretold Ruin (Ash Dawn Over Bharat)
**Visual:** High, slow aerial drift over a ruined Indian metropolis strung along a dead sacred river. Collapsed ghats, snapped bridges, shattered towers fused with temple spires, dead boats sunk in black silt. Smoke columns lean in the wind. Tiny survivors move like dust between stone and rusted metal.
**Audio:** Wind keening through ruins. A warped, time-bent siren far off. The drone climbs a half-step.
**VO / Old Sage:** "First, truth was sold. Then power was prayed to."

### 0:15-0:22 — The Foretold Poison (The Sacred River)
**Visual:** Polluted ghats at the river's edge. A mother lowers cloth into the water to wash it — and freezes as red veins pulse up through the black current beneath her hands. A child watches a dead fish drift past a half-drowned shrine, its little bell ringing on its own.
**Audio:** Water slaps stone. A child's shallow breath. Wet, organic pulsing under the mix — like something alive in the river.
**VO / Old Sage:** "The river that washed away sin... had nothing left to give."

### 0:22-0:30 — The Foretold Idols (Highways into Ruin)
**Visual:** Crane shot over broken expressways fused with toppled temple spires and rusted war machines. Scavengers cross the ash road with bundles on their backs. A burned-out tank, half-buried, has been draped in cloth and turned into a roadside shrine — offerings of bone and rust laid at its tracks.
**Audio:** Metal groans under its own weight. Faint, broken chanting from unseen survivors. Far-off war drums begin — barely there, like a heart starting.
**VO / Kali (whisper, layered, threaded through radio static):** "Take. Hoard. Survive. *Survive.*"

### 0:30-0:39 — The Foretold Market (Vice)
**Visual:** A diseased-gold bazaar of gambling, debt, and hunger. Dice tumble across a blood-stained ledger. A corrupt guard drags a man into shadow. A masked assassin watches from a balcony, still as carved stone. A priest sells painted blessings beside a locked ration cage where hands reach through the bars. A broken dharma wheel sags on a wall, half-swallowed by red veins.
**Audio:** Dice, coins, dragging chains, low crowd murmur that never resolves into words.
**VO / Kali (whisper, closer now, almost amused):** "Fear is a coin. Desire... is a leash."

### 0:39-0:49 — The Foretold Throne (Kali's Age Without a Body)
**Visual:** An empty throne of black stone and diseased gold in a ruined court. No body sits in it. Dice fall onto a war map of Bharat spread across the steps. Red-black smoke coils up off the armrests and moves like *thought* — testing the air, deciding something. Corrupt kings, warlords, and priests kneel in silhouette around it. The throne stays empty. It does not need to be filled.
**Audio:** The drums cut out. The whole room seems to inhale. One grain of sound: dice settling on stone. Smoke hisses.
**VO / Old Sage:** "Kali did not need to conquer the world."
**VO / Old Sage (a breath later, heavier):** "He taught it to kneel."

### 0:49-0:58 — The Foretold Refuge (Kailash Sanctuary + Kaka)
**Visual:** Hard smash cut — and the noise stops. Mount Kailash sanctuary: a pure river over clean stones, pine forest, snow peaks under cold clear light. Cold blue, slate, snow-white, river-cyan. Slow push-in toward the water — and in the river's surface, a young face holds calm and unaware in the reflection, while behind it, in the water, crimson smoke spreads and red-black veins crawl across the reflected sky. On a weathered shrine-stone sits KAKA — a serious, realistic black crow, blue-black sheen on his feathers, ancient eyes fixed on something we can't see.
**Audio:** Clean high-mountain wind. River water over rock. A single, sharp crow call that cuts the silence.
**VO / Old Sage:** "But the prophecy hid one truth from him..."

### 0:58-1:07 — The Foretold One (Kalki Enters the Ruined World)
**Visual:** Hard cut to a lone figure standing at the exact seam where the sanctuary's clean green dies into the ashlands — KALKI. Young Indian male, lean, alert, burdened. Rough survival cloth, a torn shawl, ash already grimed into his skin. No armor. No glow. Just a young man at the edge of a dying world. Kaka beats his wings overhead and flies on into the red storm-mist, then turns his head back, waiting. Kalki steps off the clean stone into the ash.
**Audio:** Drums return, steadier, deliberate. A low conch note swells beneath. A heartbeat enters the percussion.
**VO / Old Sage:** "...one child, raised beyond the corruption's reach—"

### 1:07-1:15 — The Foretold Recoil (The World Flinches)
**Visual:** Inside a shattered shrine choked with red-black veins. Kalki steps near a cracked relic half-fused to the wall. Blue-white light pulses once from the stone — and the corruption *recoils*, vein-tendrils peeling back across the floor and walls like burned tissue shrinking from a flame. Kalki freezes, staring at his own hand. Not triumphant. Afraid of what just answered him.
**Audio:** Stone dust trickles. A single clean harmonic tone cuts through the grime in the mix. The veins make a wet tearing sound as they pull away.
**VO / Kali (whisper, sharper, alarmed — recognition):** "...you."

### 1:15-1:24 — The Foretold Vision (The Pathways Cosmic Dream)
**Visual:** Kalki's eyes roll white — and we fall into the DREAM REALM. Deep indigo and cosmic black. He stands on a floating black-stone platform inside an impossible temple of sacred geometry and starlight. Ahead, SIX luminous paths/gates open in sequence — blue-violet to magenta to white-gold light, mandalas turning slowly in the void. Formless divine forms drift between them: a divine MALE form in pinkish electric blue, a divine FEMALE form in bluish electric pink, translucent and ethereal. Five pathways blaze. The sixth stays veiled — implied, withheld, waiting. Kaka's shadow crosses the cosmic floor.
**Audio:** The score blooms wide and weightless. Whispered Sanskrit-like textures, layered, not literal. A choir held just under the surface.
**VO / Unseen Mentor (distant, neither male nor female, kind and vast):** "Six roads will open. Five you may walk."
**VO / Unseen Mentor:** "The last... you must become."

### 1:24-1:31 — The Foretold Burden (The Choice)
**Visual:** Snap back to the real world. Extreme close on Kalki's face — red fire lighting one side, a thin blue-white line of light on the other. In his eyes flicker reflections: the poisoned river, the empty throne, starving crowds, the clean sanctuary river he left behind. The weight of all of it landing at once. His jaw tightens — and tears cut clean lines through the ash on his face. This is not a gift. It is a sentence.
**Audio:** A slow, heartbeat-like percussion, isolated. Everything else drops away around it.
**VO / Old Sage (quiet):** "And the prophecy asked of him the cruelest thing — to choose it."

### 1:31-1:40 — The Foretold Guardian (Vasuki + Claiming Pinaka)
**Visual:** A sacred cavern-shrine deep in living rock, carved with Shiva-marks, lit by a single shaft of cold light and the dull glow of red cracks held at bay. Coiled around a weapon of light and ash rests VASUKI — Lord Shiva's great serpent, vast, scaled, blue-black and ancient, eyes like cold stars. He is the guardian of PINAKA. Kalki steps in, small before him. Vasuki rises, testing him — a long, charged judgment passing between them without words. Kalki does not flinch. Then Vasuki's coils part. Kalki reaches into the light and closes his hand around PINAKA — which ripples from bow to spear to blade and back, made of light and ash, never metal. The cavern shudders.
**Audio:** A deep serpentine hiss, low and seismic, that becomes almost a word. Stone resonance. When his hand closes on the bow — a single enormous CONCH blast.
**VO / Kalki (strained, certain — first time he speaks to us):** "If it has to be carried... let it be me."
**VO / Old Sage (under it, low):** "The serpent had guarded the bow since the first age. It knew him."

### 1:40-1:48 — The Foretold Awakening (Brahma Kavach + Sharanga)
**Visual:** A battlefield of cracked earth and infected temple ruins under a blood-red sky. Kalki drops to one knee as a blue-white eruption climbs out of the ground around him. BRAHMA KAVACH ignites over his body — a luminous protective armor-aura, light woven to his frame, not a metal suit. SHARANGA burns into being at his side as a spinning chakra-shield. Pinaka locks into a drawn bow of light in his fist. The red veins rip backward across the whole battlefield, shrieking. Hope, but it costs him — his face is grief, not glory.
**Audio:** Conch blast resolves into full orchestra. War drums accelerate hard. Corruption shrieks like ten thousand roots tearing at once. Sub-bass slam.
**VO / Old Sage (rising):** "When the dark age set a crown upon its own head—"

### 1:48-1:56 — The Foretold War (Hope Learns to Fight)
**Visual:** Rapid mythic montage, grounded: survivors rise from temple settlements; a wounded fighter lifts a salvaged spear and charges; an old woman throws open hidden food stores; a young girl presses a saved green seed into cleared, dark soil and it takes. Kalki moves through the war with controlled, burdened force — blue-white light trailing through soot and blood — driving toward a Kali-aligned host massing under red storm clouds. Behind him, a single narrow road has been cleared of corruption.
**Audio:** War drums, conch, choir, metal-on-metal, human battle cries — all at full. Blue-white energy surges through the low end.
**VO / Old Sage:** "—hope had to learn to make war."

### 1:56-2:03 — The Foretold Vow (Drop to Near-Silence)
**Visual:** Kalki on a high ridge above a dead ash plain. Behind him, the narrow cleared road glows faint blue-white, people following it. Ahead: infected siege armies, burning temples, diseased-gold banners stretching to the horizon. He raises Pinaka, now a drawn bow of pure light against the red sky. Sharanga turns slowly at his side. Everything goes still around him.
**Audio:** The full mix drops out — to wind and one low, sustained conch note. Almost silence.
**VO / Kalki (low, final):** "If this age must end..."
**VO / Kalki:** "...let it end through me."

### 2:03-2:05 — The Foretold Threat (Raavan Sting)
**Visual:** Two-second flash, no warning. A colossal demon-emperor rises behind a burning fortress — RAAVAN. Soot-black and diseased-gold armor, an ancient crown, ember-red eyes opening in the dark. His many faces are only *suggested* in the smoke and shadow around his head, never counted, never clear. One vast hand grips the fortress wall; armies scatter like ash beneath him. He does not move. He only looks down — at us.
**Audio:** One monstrous, wet breath. A flame roar. A single deep metallic HIT that lands like a door slamming shut.
**VO / Kali (whisper, almost amused):** "...not yet."

### 2:05-2:09 — The Prophecy's Last Word (Title)
**Visual:** Cut to black. Then KALIYUG carved into burned stone and twisted metal, edges crusted with ash, red-black smoke curling in from the corners. Down the center of the title runs a single hair-thin crack — and through it bleeds one faint, unbroken line of blue-white light.
**Audio:** A final conch hit decaying into total silence. One last bell strike from the very beginning answers it, far away.
**VO / Old Sage (barely a whisper, the prophecy completing itself):** "...so it was written. So it begins."
**Super:** KALIYUG

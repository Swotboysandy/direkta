# Kaliyug - 2 Minute Trailer Screenplay v1

## Intent
Two-minute PS5 / Unreal Engine cinematic trailer in an ultra semi-realistic, grounded dark mythic style. The first half sells the fallen world and Kali's dominance. The second half introduces Kalki as hope entering a world already consumed by corruption, building to a 2-second Raavan tease.

## Locked Story Rules
- Kali is the demon/personification of Kali Yuga: corruption, greed, falsehood, vice, violence, and systemic rot. He is never Goddess Kali.
- Real-world scenes stay physical and grounded: ash, soot, rust, black ichor, cracked stone, diseased gold, red-black vein growths, smoke, mud, blood-stained cloth.
- Sacred geometry, cosmic mandalas, and metaphysical diagrams appear only in dream / vision shots.
- Kalki begins as a seeker in rough survival clothing, not a superhero. His divine force appears only after the midpoint.
- Raavan appears for exactly 2 seconds near the end as a colossal final-age threat.

## Runtime Structure
| Time | Function | Emotional Color |
|---:|---|---|
| 0:00-1:00 | Worldbuilding: Bharat after collapse, survivors, side characters, Kali's age | Despair, dread, moral rot |
| 1:00-1:58 | Kalki introduction, awakening, resistance, war | Hope, burden, righteous violence |
| 1:58-2:00 | Title | Mythic impact |

## Screenplay

### 0:00-0:06 - Black / The Last Age
**Visual:** Black screen. A single temple bell sways in darkness, barely visible through ash. Red-black veins creep across cracked bronze.

**Audio:** Low sub-bass rumble. Distant thunder. One slow bell strike.

**VO - Old Sage:** "An age does not end in a day."

### 0:06-0:14 - Ash Dawn Over Bharat
**Visual:** High aerial drift over a ruined Indian city along a dead sacred river. Collapsed ghats, broken bridges, shattered towers, dead boats, smoke columns. Tiny survivors move like dust between stone and metal.

**Audio:** Wind through ruins. Faint emergency sirens warped by time.

**VO - Old Sage:** "First, truth became negotiable. Then power became worship."

### 0:14-0:22 - Poisoned Sacred River
**Visual:** Polluted river ghats. A mother lowers a cloth into black water, then stops when red veins pulse beneath the surface. A child watches a dead fish float past a broken shrine.

**Audio:** Water slaps stone. Child's breath. Distant coughing.

**VO - Old Sage:** "By the time the wars came, the world had already surrendered."

### 0:22-0:30 - Highways Become Ruins
**Visual:** Crane shot over broken highways fused with temple spires and rusted war machines. Scavengers cross the ash road with bundles. A burned-out tank is used as a shrine.

**Audio:** Metal groans. Soft chanting from unseen survivors.

**Kali Whisper, layered through radio static:** "Take. Hoard. Survive."

### 0:30-0:38 - Survivor Fires Under Broken Towers
**Visual:** A temple settlement under collapsed overpasses. Fires burn in steel drums. A wounded fighter binds his arm. An old woman paints a cracked dharma wheel on a wall. A young girl hides a clean seed in a tin box.

**Audio:** Fire crackle. Cloth snapping in wind. Low drums begin, very far away.

**VO - Old Sage:** "Some still prayed. Some still protected. Most simply endured."

### 0:38-0:48 - Markets of Vice
**Visual:** Diseased-gold bazaar of gambling, debt, propaganda, and hunger. Dice roll across a blood-stained ledger. A corrupt guard drags a man away. A masked assassin watches from a balcony. A priest sells blessings beside a ration cage.

**Audio:** Dice, coins, chains, crowd murmurs.

**Kali Whisper:** "Fear is a currency. Desire is a leash."

### 0:48-0:56 - Kali's Throne Without a Body
**Visual:** Empty black stone and diseased-gold throne inside a ruined court. Dice fall onto a war map. Red-black smoke coils like thought. Corrupt kings, warlords, and priests kneel in silhouette. The throne remains empty.

**Audio:** Drums stop. The room inhales.

**VO - Old Sage:** "Kali did not conquer the world."

**VO - Old Sage:** "He taught it to kneel."

### 0:56-1:00 - The Place Kept Clean
**Visual:** Smash cut to Kailash sanctuary. Pure river. Snow peaks. Pine forest. A young boy's bare feet stand at the water's edge. Kaka, a serious black crow, watches from an old stone shrine.

**Audio:** Clean wind. River water. One crow call.

**VO - Old Sage:** "But one truth was hidden."

### 1:00-1:08 - Kalki Enters the Ruined World
**Visual:** Hard cut to adult Kalki at the boundary of the sanctuary and the ashlands. He wears torn survival cloth, ash on his skin, no armor. Kaka flies ahead into red storm mist. Kalki steps after him.

**Audio:** Drums return, now steadier. Low conch note under the music.

**VO - Old Sage:** "Raised outside the corruption..."

### 1:08-1:16 - The Relic Recoils
**Visual:** Inside a shattered shrine. Kalki approaches a cracked relic. Blue-white light pulses once. Nearby red-black veins recoil from him like burned tissue. Kalki is startled, not triumphant.

**Audio:** Stone dust falls. A clean harmonic tone cuts through the noise.

**VO - Old Sage:** "...so he could see the world without its lies."

### 1:16-1:25 - Vision: The Mirror of Selves
**Visual:** Dream/vision realm. Kalki stands on a floating black-stone platform inside a vast cosmic temple of sacred geometry and starlight. Broken mirrors reveal him as child, seeker, warrior, and avatar fragments. Kaka's shadow crosses the floor.

**Audio:** Music opens wide. Whispered Sanskrit-like textures, not literal chanting.

**Unseen Mentor:** "You were not born to escape the age."

**Kalki:** "Then tell me what I am."

### 1:25-1:34 - The Burden
**Visual:** Close on Kalki's face in the real world, lit by red fire on one side and blue-white light on the other. Reflections of ruined people, war maps, and the sanctuary river flicker in his eyes.

**Audio:** Heartbeat-like percussion.

**Unseen Mentor:** "A choice."

**Beat.**

**Unseen Mentor:** "Then a weapon."

### 1:34-1:44 - Awakening in Ash
**Visual:** Battlefield of cracked earth and infected temple ruins. Kalki falls to one knee as a blue-white eruption surrounds him. Brahma Kavach appears as luminous protective armor aura, not a metal suit. Pinaka forms from light and ash, shifting bow to spear to blade. Sharanga burns into being as a chakra-shield. Red veins pull back across the ground.

**Audio:** Conch blast. Drums accelerate. Corruption shrieks like tearing roots.

**VO - Old Sage:** "When darkness crowns itself king..."

### 1:44-1:52 - War Against the Age
**Visual:** Rapid montage. Survivors rise from temple settlements. The wounded fighter leads a charge with a salvaged spear. The old woman opens hidden food stores. The young girl plants the saved seed in cleared ground. Kalki moves through battle with controlled, burdened force, pushing toward a Kali-aligned army under red storm clouds.

**Audio:** War drums, metal impacts, human battle cries. Blue-white energy surges through the mix.

**VO - Old Sage:** "...hope must learn to fight."

### 1:52-1:56 - Kalki's Vow
**Visual:** Kalki stands on a ridge above a dead ash plain. Behind him, a narrow road has cleared through the corruption. Ahead: infected armies, siege machines, burning temples, diseased banners. He raises Pinaka. Sharanga glows at his side.

**Audio:** Everything drops except wind and the low conch.

**Kalki:** "If this age must end..."

**Kalki:** "...it ends through me."

### 1:56-1:58 - Raavan Tease
**Visual:** Two-second flash. Colossal Raavan rises behind a burning fortress: soot-black and diseased-gold armor, ember eyes, ancient crown. Many faces are suggested only through smoke and shadow. His hand grips the fortress wall as armies scatter below.

**Audio:** One monstrous breath. Flame roar. A deep metallic hit.

### 1:58-2:00 - Title
**Visual:** Title card: KALIYUG carved in burned stone and metal. Ash drifts. Red-black smoke curls at the edges. A faint blue-white line of light breaks through the center crack.

**Audio:** Final conch hit into silence.

**Super:** KALIYUG

## Trailer Rhythm Notes
- First half camera language: wide, slow, observational, oppressive. The world is the main character.
- Second half camera language: closer, faster, more vertical and mythic, but still grounded.
- Kali's dominance should be present in nearly every real-world shot through red-black veins, dice, broken dharma wheels, propaganda, debt, hunger, corrupted authority, and diseased gold.
- Kalki's hope should feel costly, not bright or clean. Blue-white light does not erase the horror; it gives people a reason to stand inside it.
- Raavan must be a sting, not a full reveal. Do not over-explain him in this trailer.
